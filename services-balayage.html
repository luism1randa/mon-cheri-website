Balayage

Balayage is a color technique where hair is painted freehand. Unlike highlights, balayage is hand-painted onto strands of hair, giving you a unique touch. Whether you want to go for a natural look or create something more bold, balayage is the perfect way to change up your style.

While balayage is great for adding some sassiness back into your look, it also helps with blending grays and is less damaging on your hair than traditional highlights. If you're tired of the same old hair color, it's time to try balayage for a refreshing new look!
